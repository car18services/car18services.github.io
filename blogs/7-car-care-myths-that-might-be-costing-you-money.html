7 Car Care Myths That Might Be Costing You Money

August 5, 2024 1,950 views By Car18 Team

Many car owners follow advice passed down through generations or shared online, but not all of it is accurate. Some common car care myths can actually damage your vehicle or cost you unnecessary money. Here are 7 widespread misconceptions our Car18 experts want to debunk to help you maintain your car properly and save money in the long run.

Myth #1: Dish Soap is Fine for Washing Cars

Many believe any soap will do for car washing, leading them to use household dish soap.

The Truth:

Dish soap is designed to strip grease and will remove your car's protective wax or sealant. This leaves your paint vulnerable to UV rays, oxidation, and contaminants.

Potential Cost: ₹3,000-₹8,000 annually in premature wax/sealant reapplication

Car18 Pro Tip: Use a pH-neutral car shampoo specifically formulated for automotive paint. Our Car18 Wash Kit includes a premium shampoo that cleans without compromising protection.

Myth #2: More Detergent Means a Cleaner Car

The assumption that extra shampoo or stronger cleaners will get your car cleaner.

The Truth:

Excess detergent leaves residues that attract dirt and can damage rubber seals and trim. Strong cleaners may strip protection and dry out materials.

Potential Cost: ₹2,000-₹5,000 in premature interior/exterior trim replacement

Car18 Pro Tip: Follow product dilution ratios exactly. Our professional detailing services use precisely measured solutions for optimal cleaning without damage.

Myth #3: Waxing Too Often is Bad for Paint

Some believe waxing more than twice a year can damage a car's finish.

The Truth:

Regular waxing (every 2-3 months) provides essential protection. Modern waxes can be applied as often as needed without buildup issues.

Potential Cost: ₹15,000-₹40,000 in paint correction from unprotected exposure

Car18 Pro Tip: Consider ceramic or graphene coatings for longer-lasting protection with less frequent applications needed.

Myth #4: Car Washes With Brushes Clean Better

The belief that stiff brushes at automatic car washes provide superior cleaning.

The Truth:

These brushes trap dirt and cause micro-scratches and swirl marks that dull your paint over time.

Potential Cost: ₹8,000-₹25,000 for paint correction to remove swirl marks

Car18 Pro Tip: Opt for touchless washes or our mobile hand-wash service using soft microfiber mitts that won't scratch your paint.

Myth #5: You Should Wash Your Car in Direct Sunlight

Many think washing in sunlight helps the car dry faster.

The Truth:

Sunlight causes water and soap to dry too quickly, leaving spots and potentially baking contaminants into the paint.

Potential Cost: ₹3,000-₹10,000 for water spot removal and paint decontamination

Car18 Pro Tip: Always wash in shade or during cooler parts of the day. Our mobile service brings pop-up shades for perfect washing conditions anywhere.

Myth #6: Interior Cleaning is Just About Appearance

The notion that interior cleaning is purely cosmetic and can be skipped.

The Truth:

Dirt and debris accelerate wear on upholstery and can damage mechanisms. Spills can cause permanent stains and odors if not treated promptly.

Potential Cost: ₹10,000-₹50,000 in premature seat reupholstering or carpet replacement

Car18 Pro Tip: Schedule professional interior detailing every 3-6 months. Our Deep Clean service removes allergens and preserves your interior's value.

Myth #7: Premium Fuel Cleans Your Engine

The belief that higher-octane fuel automatically cleans your engine better.

The Truth:

Octane rating relates to burn resistance, not cleaning ability. Many regular fuels contain the same detergents as premium versions.

Potential Cost: ₹15,000+ annually in unnecessary fuel upgrades

Car18 Pro Tip: Use the octane level recommended in your owner's manual. For proper engine cleaning, consider our fuel system cleaning service every 15,000 km.

How Much Could These Myths Be Costing You?

If you've been following several of these myths, you could be wasting ₹50,000-₹1,50,000 over 5 years in:

  • Premature paint correction and detailing
  • Unnecessary part replacements
  • Excessive product usage
  • Higher fuel costs
  • Reduced resale value

Smart Car Care Strategy:

Invest in proper maintenance through our Car18 subscription plans. For less than the cost of repairing damage from these myths, you can keep your car in showroom condition with regular professional care.

The Bottom Line

Proper car care isn't just about following traditions or assumptions - it's about understanding what actually protects your investment. By avoiding these common myths, you'll save money, maintain your car's value, and enjoy a better driving experience. When in doubt, consult our Car18 experts for advice tailored to your specific vehicle and driving conditions.
